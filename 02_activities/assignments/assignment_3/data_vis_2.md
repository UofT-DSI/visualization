# Age Distribution by Heart Disease Status

This bar plot visualizes the relationship between age and heart disease status in the dataset. Key features of the visualization:

- The x-axis shows 6 age categories: 20, 30, 40, 50, 60, and 70
- The y-axis represents the age counts of individuals per category in the entire dataset
- Each bar shows the count of individuals with or without heart disease
- Colors are automatically assigned to distinguish between the two groups
- Legend helps understand which is 0 or 1

This visualization helps us quickly understand the number of individuals in each age group/category with or without heart disease.
